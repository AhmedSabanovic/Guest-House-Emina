Guest House Emina, located 9 km from Sarajevo Memorial for Children Killed during Siege, features free Wi-Fi throughout the venue. The accommodation comprises 8 rooms. There's also a convenience of free parking on site. It is 4 km from the centre of Sarajevo. Guests can also visit Eternal flame, which is a 10-minute drive away.Olimpijski muzej is also located near the hotel. Mercator Grand centar is an easy walk away.

Some rooms also have heating, an own balcony and a trouser press. An electric kettle, refrigerator and kitchenware are also included. Guests can enjoy their morning meal at the bar.The nearest bus stop is Butmir I, it is only 300 metres away.
