Welcome to Butmir

Nestled in the peaceful and historic neighborhood of Butmir, Ilidža, Guest House Emina offers a serene sanctuary just moments from Sarajevo's most captivating landmarks. We are perfectly situated for travelers seeking both relaxation and exploration.

Immerse yourself in history with a short walk to the famous Tunnel of Hope (Tunel Spasa), or embrace nature at the stunning Vrelo Bosne springs and the tree-lined Great Alley (Velika Aleja). For relaxation, the Ilidža Thermal Riviera is just a stone's throw away.

With Sarajevo International Airport practically at our doorstep, your journey is effortless. Our villa features 8 elegantly appointed rooms with private balconies, modern amenities, and a lush garden setting that feels like a home away from home.
